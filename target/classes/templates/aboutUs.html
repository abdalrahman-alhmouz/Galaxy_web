WHO WE ARE

Established in 2015, Galaxy is an IT based non- profit organization dedicated to driving economic growth by undertaking various programs and activities for Children, Youth, and Women and other marginalized segments of the society, by working on developing their skills and improving their standards of living we focus on educational and digital uplifting of these groups through developing and conducting related training and educational activities. Galaxy is keen on facilitating sustainable development by creating balance between Society, Economy and the Environment through actively working with potential contributors, government entities, service providers, literacy centers, universities and colleges to bring knowledge and expertise to where they are mostly needed. Driven to help people in vulnerable technological situations, we are working on spreading knowledge related to using computers through providing them with recycled devices after being prepared and tested by fresh graduates as part of their work experience thus focusing on communities in need for being aware of technological solutions.

OUR VISION

We seek a world where all Women, Youth and Children; even in the most remote areas; will be able to hold the power to create a better opportunity for themselves and others.

OUR MISSION

Galaxy is an IT based, non-profit organization with a driving passion towards helping all segments of the community to obtain an immediate and lasting sustainable technological solutions and services that will create a supportive, productive and equal future for everyone.
